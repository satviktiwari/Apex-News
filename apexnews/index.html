About Us

Apεx News is a Free, Open-Source, Brief-News delivery Platform which calls News Headlines and Contents in the form of API Packages from various News Sources. Our Primary News Sources include The Times of India, The Hindu, Inshorts, New York Times, The Diplomat, ANI, Indian Express, NDTV & BBC. Apεx News is a Non-Profit group and we do not claim copyrights on any information that is displayed on our Website.

Crisp Headlines and Short Description

We Aim to provide News in least possible words. The Crisp Headline in less than 10 words allows the user to understand the Topic smoothly and the whole Content Story is provided to the reader in less than 30 words. A 5 minute read feels sufficient to get the understanding of events occuring across the Globe

No Fake News

Keeping a Neutral and Centric approach, Apεx News respects every Ideology. Without any Biasness & Hatred towards any Individual or Organization and with complete Fact Check, we provide only Authentic Information to our Readers.
